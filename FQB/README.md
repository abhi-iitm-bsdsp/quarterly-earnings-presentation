## Analysis & Key Findings
- Q1 and Q2 are **well below industry benchmark**, indicating excess inventory and storage inefficiencies.
- Q3 and Q4 are **above benchmark**, suggesting potential over-corrections or seasonal fluctuations.
- Overall average (6.88) is below target of 8, showing room for optimization.

## Business Implications
- Low turnover in Q1 and Q2 increases **holding costs** and reduces cash flow.
- Over-corrections in Q3/Q4 may cause stockouts or supply chain stress.
- The trend indicates **inconsistent inventory management** across quarters.

## Recommendations
- **Optimize supply chain and demand forecasting**:
  - Implement predictive analytics for inventory demand.
  - Adjust procurement schedules based on seasonal trends.
  - Align storage and logistics operations to reduce costs.
- Aim for a **consistent turnover ratio of 8**, minimizing quarterly variance.
